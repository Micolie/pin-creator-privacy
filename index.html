Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Pin Creator app ("the App"), developed by Micolie, handles information.

1. Personal Use Only

This app is for personal use only. It is not distributed to the public and is not available for download by third parties.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The only data accessed is the Pinterest account of the developer (Micolie) for the purpose of creating pins.

3. Pinterest API

The App uses the Pinterest API to create pins on behalf of the developer's own Pinterest account. No data is shared with third parties beyond what is required by the Pinterest platform.

4. Cookies

The App does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
